Why Do I Need to Create an Account?

To request an item through Interlibrary Loan (ILL), you must first create an account in the library's system.
By creating an account:

  • 1. You will not have to supply your personal information again when making future requests.
  • 2. The library will be able to notify you promptly via email when your request is available.
  • 3. You will have secure access to information about your requests. You will also be able to review the list of items you have requested in the past.
