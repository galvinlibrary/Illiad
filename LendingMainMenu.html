Request Types


Use this button to request a photocopy of an article within a larger work, or to request a patent. Examples include an article within a journal, a paper  within a conference proceedings, or a single chapter within an anthology.


Use this button to request the loan of an entire work. Examples include books, entire conference proceedings, and loans of microfilm reels and microfiche.
